About Us

Mamasaheb Mohol College, also known as Pune Zilha Shikshan Mandal's
Mamasaheb Mohol College, is an educational institution located in Pune,
Maharashtra. It's affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and offers a
range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in arts, commerce, and
science. The college is known for its commitment to academic excellence and
providing a nurturing environment for students.
